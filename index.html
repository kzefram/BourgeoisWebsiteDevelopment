About Karen Bourgeois

Hello! I'm Karen Bourgeois, and my journey into the world of web development began in 2020, sparked by the simple curiosity of my own child asking how coding worked. That initial spark ignited a passion that I've been cultivating ever since. My education includes formal training through UNB, practical experience from Startup Support Plus, and countless hours of self-directed learning. This diverse background has equipped me with a robust understanding of modern web technologies.

I specialize in crafting custom digital experiences, ranging from meticulously hand-coded websites to flexible and user-friendly platforms built with WordPress (Divi). I also create efficient, no-maintenance static landing pages, perfect for focused campaigns. What truly drives me is tailoring each project to meet a client's unique vision and business goals. For my WordPress clients, I also offer ongoing SEO upkeep to ensure their online presence consistently performs and reaches the right audience. Let's build something great together!
